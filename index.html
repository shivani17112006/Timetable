      🌞 Skin Care
1. Tan Removal
Best Combo: Turmeric powder + Milk + Curd
Recipe:

1 tsp turmeric powder

2 tsp curd

1 tsp milk
How to use:

Mix into a paste, apply to tanned areas, leave for 15 min, wash with lukewarm water.

Use 2–3 times a week.

2. Face Glower
Best Combo: Coffee powder + Milk + Banana
Recipe:

1 tsp coffee powder

1–2 tsp milk

2–3 mashed banana slices
How to use:

Mix well, apply to face, leave for 15–20 min, wash with cool water.

Use once or twice a week for fresh glow.

3. Moisturiser
Best Combo: Aloe vera gel + Almond oil
Recipe:

2 tsp aloe vera gel

3–4 drops almond oil
How to use:

Blend and store in a small container, use daily as a natural moisturiser.

4. Eye Makeup/Shadow Remover
Best Combo: Almond oil + Milk
Recipe:

Mix 1 tsp almond oil with 1 tsp milk.

Dip cotton, gently wipe eyes, then wash with plain water.

💇 Hair Care
1. Hair Smoother
Best Combo: Aloe vera gel + Almond oil
Recipe:

2 tbsp aloe vera gel

1 tbsp almond oil

Apply to hair length for 30 min before washing.

2. Hair Glowing Oil
Best Combo: Almond oil + Boiled rice water
Recipe:

Warm 2 tbsp almond oil slightly.

Mix with 3 tbsp cooled boiled rice water.

Massage scalp for 5–10 min, leave 1 hour, wash.

3. Hair Conditioner
Best Combo: Curd + Aloe vera gel + Banana
Recipe:

3 tbsp curd

2 tbsp aloe vera gel

2–3 mashed banana slices

Apply to hair after shampooing, leave 15 min, rinse.

4. Hair Growing Oil
Best Combo: Almond oil + Beetroot juice + Carrot juice
Recipe:

Warm 3 tbsp almond oil.

Add 1 tbsp beetroot juice + 1 tbsp carrot juice.

Massage scalp for 10 min, leave overnight or at least 1 hour before washing.

🍀 For Overall Hair & Skin Health
Rice water – Use as hair rinse once a week, makes hair strong.

Cucumber – Eat raw or apply juice to face for hydration.

Beetroot & carrot juice – Drinking improves skin glow & hair roots.
